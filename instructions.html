How It Works

Invisible Decisions is an interactive experience about the small choices you make throughout the day, especially when managing type 1 diabetes.

1. Read each scenario

You will move through connected everyday situations, such as waking up, eating, travelling, socialising, and dealing with pressure.

2. Make a choice

Each option affects your blood sugar, insulin, stress, and irritation levels. Some choices may make later decisions harder.

3. Watch the impact

Your decisions build up over time. The aim is not to “win”, but to notice how many hidden decisions happen in one day.
